About Iron Man

Iron Man is one of the most iconic superheroes in the Marvel Universe. His real name is Tony Stark, a brilliant engineer and inventor who created the Iron Man suit to save his life and protect the world from threats.

Through intelligence, technology, and determination, Tony Stark became a symbol of innovation and heroism.

Key Information

  • Real Name: Tony Stark
  • Occupation: Inventor & Businessman
  • Company: Stark Industries
  • Team: Avengers
  • First Appearance: 1963

Technology & Innovation
